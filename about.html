About Zachary

Originally from Denver, CO, Zach wrote his first programs on his TI-83 graphing calculator in middle school. His passion was rekindled while he was working as a game designer for Adam Poots, as he found himself constantly yearning for software that didn't exist to help him analyze game mechanics and crunch playtesting data. Soon thereafter, he enrolled in App Academy to help make the transition from part-time hobbyist to full-fledged software developer.
